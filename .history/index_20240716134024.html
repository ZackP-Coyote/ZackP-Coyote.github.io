My Projects

FoodFate

Food Fate is a web application designed to assist users in finding dinner recommendations within a specified area.
Users can enter their desired location, set parameters such as distance and food preferences to receive three random restaurant suggestions.

Find My MicroWave

A mobile android app that helps California State University students and faculty locate useful campus resources.

DoomStrike

“DoomStrike” is an online third-person shooter with characters having unique traits giving the player the opportunity
to play at their own strengths in shooter games involving different guns with their own specific loadouts. “DoomStrike”
sets itself apart from other shooters because there are not many competitive third-person shooters that offer a
different play-style and abilities for each weapon.
